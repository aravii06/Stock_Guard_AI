To build "Stock Guard AI" successfully as a Postgraduate project, you need to structure your time between Learning (R&D) and Building (Coding).Here is your complete Master Roadmap. Focus only on these 3 pillars.Module A: Financial Forensics (20% of effort)Goal: Understand how to spot a lie on a chart.Candlestick Patterns: Learn to read "Marubozu" (strong candle) vs "Doji" (uncertainty).Volume Analysis: Deep dive into "Volume Price Analysis (VPA)."Key Concept: Divergence. (Price goes up, Volume goes down = Scam).Market Structure: Learn "Support and Resistance." Scammers often pump a stock past its "Resistance" level to trap buyers.The "Operator" Modus Operandi: Study how "Circular Trading" works (buying from yourself to fake volume).Module B: The AI "Brain" - LSTM (50% of effort)Goal: Understand why we use LSTM, not just simple Regression.Time-Series Data: Learn why stock prices are a "Sequence" (like a movie), not independent numbers (like a lottery).LSTM Architecture: Study the 3 gates:Forget Gate: "What old info is useless?"Input Gate: "What new info is important?"Output Gate: "What should I predict?"Normalization: Why inputs must be 0-1 (MinMax Scaling).Overfitting: The biggest risk. When your AI memorizes the past but fails at the future.Module C: NLP & Social Engineering (30% of effort)Goal: Understand how text predicts price.Sentiment Analysis: How to turn text ("Buy!!!") into a number (0.9).Bot Detection: Study patterns of spam (e.g., 100 messages in 1 minute with the same exact text).Telethon Library: How to scrape Telegram without getting banned.Part 2: The Project Timeline (The Schedule)Assuming a standard 8-Week (2 Month) intensive timeline for a PG project.PhaseWeekTask NameWhat you will doStatus1Week 1Data Engineering• Build data_collector.py (Completed)  • Build data_processor.py (Completed)  • Study: OHLC & Volume basics.✅ DONE2Week 2The LSTM Brain• Write model_trainer.py.  • Train the AI on Reliance data.  • Study: LSTM Architecture.⬅️ YOU ARE HERE3Week 3Validation• Test the AI.
